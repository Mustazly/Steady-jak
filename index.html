Read our story

We've started the Food War between East and West Zone

The owner of the cafe is the one who love the Western Food. But his never forget about his culture and the food that he eat in his state in Malaysia which is Sarawak. From that, owner of the restaurant make an idea build the West and East food in one restaurant which London City food as the main Western food will be served. The name of the restaurant is combination between Sarawak and London itself.
